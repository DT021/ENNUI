Welcome to ENNUI
  • To build a neural network:
    • Create layers and activations.
    • Snap activations into the hole at the bottom of the layer.
    • Change parameters of a selected layer in the right panel.
    • Connect layers by clicking the black dot on a selected layer then click on another layer.
  • Train an architecture on MNIST data clicking train in the upper right.
  • Click tabs on the left to view training progress and see visualizations.
  • There are template options in the bottom left to help you get started.
